## 6. Executive Summary

Across **1,122 conversations from April 28 – May 19, 2026**, volume averaged ~62 chats/day with a sharp surge in **week 2 (May 4–9, 553 chats; peak 99 on May 7)**. **Bonus solicitation dominates at ~52% of all contacts** — overwhelmingly repetitive, low-complexity requests ideal for automation. The second-largest category is **withdrawals (~17%)**, where delays beyond 24-72h are generating significant frustration, repeat contacts, and at least one public complaint on casino.guru regarding a €1,220 "balance correction." **Critical risks**: (1) recurring "balance correction" / missing-deposit complaints that look like reconciliation failures and pose **regulatory and reputational exposure**; (2) **VIP host unresponsiveness** (Daniel, Aline, Telegram) driving high-value players to public live chat in distress; (3) **responsible-gambling lapses** — customers report being re-solicited after self-exclusion, with multiple emotional pleas ("addiction", "Je suis tellement triste"). **Top recommendation**: Deploy a chatbot to handle the ~55-60% of automatable traffic (bonus eligibility checks, cashback lookups, KYC status, password reset, affiliate-FS crediting), freeing agents to (a) clear the withdrawal backlog within a guaranteed 24h SLA, (b) staff VIP responsiveness, and (c) properly handle closure/self-exclusion cases — which must never be automated.
